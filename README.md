# The Role of Generative AI in Cybersecurity Knowledge
## A Case Study on Phishing Attacks

### Overview
This research study explores the role of Generative AI in cybersecurity knowledge production, focusing on phishing attacks. The study evaluates AI-generated content and performs a comparative assessment between different generative AI models.

### Research Objectives
- Analyze phishing as a cybersecurity threat
- Evaluate the reliability of AI-generated content
- Compare different Generative AI models
- Validate findings through academic literature

### Methodology
- AI-assisted content generation
- Comparative AI evaluation
- Bibliographic validation (ENISA, academic journals)

### Key Topics
- Social Engineering
- Spear Phishing
- MFA
- Zero Trust
- AI-based detection systems

### Academic Context
University research project (Spring Semester 2025)
